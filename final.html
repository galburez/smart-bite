Smart Bite: Smarter Meals, Healthier Living

Smart Bite is a personalized meal planning app designed to simplify healthy eating. It helps users create dynamic meal plans tailored to their dietary preferences, cooking skills, and nutritional goals. With features like recipe discovery, grocery list generation, and nutritional tracking, Smart Bite makes it easy to plan, prepare, and enjoy meals that fit your lifestyle.

Checkout the Main Features:

Your Plan

The Your Plan tab helps you build a dynamic, personalized meal plan tailored to your favorite recipes and preferences.

Recipe Book

The Recipe Book lets you create personalized cookbooks with your favorite recipes and discover new ones to try.

Resources

The Resources tab is where you can find helpful guides and videos that will elevate your skills and meal prepping ability.

Explore the User Flow:

This is the user flow for a new user who is trying to visit the Recipe Book tab, and see his current recipe books and new suggested recipes. They go through a basic on boarding quiz followed by a basic tour of the application. From there the user is able to freely visit the recipe book tab. Use the next and back buttons to navigate.
